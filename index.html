Welcome to Surplus 2 Serve

What is Surplus 2 Server?

  • Surplus 2 Serve is a platform that connects individuals, businesses and warehouses with surplus primary agricultural products to NGOs and food banks that can distribute it to those in need.
  • It doesn't matter if you are private or public organization, just let it go smoothly before it rots

How it works?

  1. Donor submits product information as per the requirements
  2. Our ML-integrated System predicts shelf-life of the products and tag them according to their spoilage rate
  3. NGOs / Food banks request the at-risk items they require
