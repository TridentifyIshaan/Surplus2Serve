Welcome to Surplus 2 Serve

Connecting surplus food to those in need, powered by technology and compassion.

We Support the Sustainable Development Goals

Our mission aligns with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals, particularly Goal 2: Zero Hunger and Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production.

By redistributing surplus food, we aim to reduce food waste and ensure that no one goes hungry.

Join us in making a difference!

What is Surplus 2 Serve?

  • Surplus 2 Serve connects individuals, businesses, and warehouses with surplus agricultural products to NGOs and food banks that can distribute them to those in need.
  • Whether you are a private or public organization, let your surplus reach those who need it before it goes to waste.

How it works?

  1. Donor submits product information as per the requirements.
  2. Our ML-integrated system predicts shelf-life and tags products according to spoilage rate.
  3. NGOs / Food banks request the at-risk items they require.
