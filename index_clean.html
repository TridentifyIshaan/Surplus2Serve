About Surplus2Serve

Transforming food waste into hope through intelligent technology and community collaboration

Our Mission

Surplus2Serve is a revolutionary platform that bridges the gap between food surplus and hunger. We connect individuals, businesses, and warehouses with surplus agricultural products to NGOs and food banks, ensuring that nutritious food reaches those who need it most before it goes to waste.

Fighting hunger in communities
Reducing food waste globally
Using AI for smart distribution

How It Works

1. Submit Information

Donors submit detailed product information including quantity, storage conditions, and location.

2. AI Analysis

Our machine learning model predicts shelf-life and spoilage risk, prioritizing urgent items.

3. Smart Matching

NGOs and food banks request items based on need, with priority given to at-risk products.

4. Distribution

Coordinated delivery ensures food reaches communities before spoilage occurs.
